# Format: STATE > TRIGGERS > ACTIONS > NOTES

# ============================================================
# GLOBAL RULES (apply to ALL states)
# ============================================================

GLOBAL:
  CLIMBABLE: walls, fences, large flat surfaces, large pipes
  NOT CLIMBABLE: nothing excluded — all BlockAll surfaces are climbable
  OBSTACLE HANDLING: StepUp first (MaxStepHeight=200 UU). If StepUp fails, brief climb.
                     Full climb only for walls >= 150 UU (MinClimbWallHeight).
                     NO VAULT SYSTEM (removed — was dead code, looked like hopping).
  CLIMB CREST: push over wall top scaled by wall height (ClimbStartZ → ClimbTargetZ).
               Short fences get small pushes, tall walls get full pushes.
  LAUNCH: short ballistic arc ONLY (like L4D Hunter). Max height ~3m above ground. Never fly.
  DAMAGE SOURCE: ApplyDamage (single target) or ApplyRadialDamage (AoE)
  GUNSHOT: ALL crawlers in level alerted instantly → Hunt. No distance limit.
  INDOOR/OUTDOOR: upward trace detects ceiling. Indoor = 3D distance. Outdoor = 2D distance.
  POST-CREST: after climbing over a wall/fence, creature redirects 180° AWAY from climb axis.
              Never re-climbs the same obstacle. Prevents climb-drop-climb loops.
  LANDING ON PLAYER: never land directly on player. All lunges/pounces offset 200 UU in front.
  NO UNPROVOKED VERTICAL: creatures NEVER hop/bounce vertically during ground movement.
              Upward Z velocity is ZERO during ground pursuit. Only climbing and launching go up.
  PENETRATION: during climbing, push UPWARD only through wall lips. Never phase horizontally.
               On ground, push OUT along impact normal.
  DEATH: freeze in place (all tick/anim/timelines/timers stopped) + drop to ground via trace.
         NO ragdoll, NO tilt, NO leg explosion. Corpse despawns after 30s.
  SPAWN: hidden during InitialPatrolDelay (2s). Prevents visible settling (body bob, IK init).

# ============================================================
# STATE: PATROL (idle, no threat detected)
# ============================================================

STATE: Patrol
  SPEED: 250 UU/s
  BEHAVIOR:
    - Ground wander within WanderRadius (1500 UU) of home position
    - Random direction changes, pause at destinations (0.5-1.5s)
    - Occasionally climb a wall/fence if nearby (not every wall — maybe 30% chance)
    - After cresting a wall, redirect 180° away and continue ground patrol
    - NOT a "climb everything" machine — walls are part of the environment, not the goal
    - If spawned on elevated surface (walkway, platform), patrol along it — don't freeze
  TRANSITIONS:
    - Player detected (LOS + within 3000 UU) → Hunt
    - Gunshot heard → Hunt (ALL crawlers, level-wide)
  NOTES:
    - Should feel like an animal in its territory, not a robot
    - Ground movement is primary, walls are incidental

# ============================================================
# STATE: HUNT (actively pursuing player)
# ============================================================

STATE: Hunt
  SPEED: 300 UU/s
  BEHAVIOR:
    - Direct pursuit toward player on ground
    - Uses walls/fences as shortcuts (climb over, not around)
    - GAZE FREEZE: ONLY when creature is ON A WALL (climbing). Freezes when player looks.
    - ON GROUND: NO freeze. Direct charge. No hesitation.
    - After 2s continuous LOS on ground → lunge or scurry attack (aggressive, not passive)
    - Being fired upon → immediate Hunt + charge (gunshot response)
    - Hearing: tracks player within 1500 UU even without LOS
    - Retargets every 3s if not making progress
  TRANSITIONS:
    - Within attack range + same Z level → Attack (alternating drop/lunge)
    - Elevated 400+ UU above player (max 800 UU) → Perch → Pounce
    - Lose sight for 10s → Stalk
  POUNCE RULES:
    - Perch (freeze) for 2.5s before pouncing
    - Max pounce height: 800 UU above player. Higher = don't pounce, come down first
    - Pounce arc: like L4D Hunter. Short, fast, aggressive. Not a sky dive.
    - Land 200 UU in front of player, not on top
    - 30 damage on landing proximity
    - 10s cooldown after landing
  NOTES:
    - One pace. No dashing, no speed bursts.
    - The gaze freeze on WALLS is the horror. On ground, creature is aggressive.

# ============================================================
# STATE: STALK (lost sight, repositioning)
# ============================================================

STATE: Stalk
  SPEED: 280 UU/s
  BEHAVIOR:
    - Move to elevated position near last known player location
    - Prefer walls/fences for elevation (use WallMap)
    - Freeze when player looks at creature (on wall or ground — stalk is sneaky)
    - Stare aggro: if player stares for 2s → Hunt
    - Proximity aggro: player within 500 UU → Hunt
    - Repositions laterally, not just vertically
  TRANSITIONS:
    - Re-sight player → Hunt (skip Alert, no delay)
    - Player stares 2s → Hunt
    - Player within 500 UU → Hunt
    - Elevated 200+ UU above player (after 8s stalk time) → Perch → Pounce
    - 30s timeout with no re-sight → Patrol
  POUNCE RULES:
    - Same as Hunt pounce but lower threshold (200 UU vs 400 UU)
    - Same max height cap (800 UU)
    - Must have been stalking for 8s minimum before pounce allowed
  NOTES:
    - This is the "wall crawler" state — creature should BE on walls
    - Closing distance without player noticing is the goal
    - Max stalk height: 800 UU above player (no climbing to the moon)

# ============================================================
# STATE: ATTACK (in combat range)
# ============================================================

STATE: Attack
  BEHAVIOR:
    - Alternating: drop, lunge, drop, lunge (not random, not distance-based)
    - DROP: Rear up (0.3s wind-up), then ground impact, 25 damage
    - LUNGE: Short forward leap toward player, 25 damage on proximity (250 UU), land 200 UU in front
  TRANSITIONS:
    - Attack complete → Hunt (immediate, barely pauses)
  COOLDOWN: 1.5s between attacks
  NOTES:
    - Attacks should feel relentless while in range
    - No deliberation — creature is committed once it reaches you
    - Lunge should NOT be a fly mechanism. Short arc, close to ground.
