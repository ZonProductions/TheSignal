### TICKET-039 — Menu UI Design Language + Fonts
- Status: DONE
- Priority: HIGH
- Category: Art
- Notes:
  - **Design influences:** Silent Hill 2 (minimalism, literary serif, negative space) + Resident Evil 2 Remake (industrial condensed sans-serif, dark contrast, clean layout).
  - **Core principles:**
    - Darkness IS the design. No decorative panels, borders, or chrome. Text floats on black.
    - Negative space communicates isolation. Don't fill the screen.
    - Animations are slow and deliberate. Nothing snaps. Everything breathes.
    - Color palette is almost monochrome. Red is earned — used sparingly for selection/danger.
  - **Color palette:**
    - Background: near-black (#0A0A0A to #121212)
    - Primary text: off-white (#D4D4D4) — never pure white
    - Muted text: (#888888) for secondary info, (#666666) for tertiary
    - Greyed out: (#444444) for unavailable options
    - Accent: blood red (#8B1A1A) for selection state, active highlights
    - Hover: rust (#6B3A2A) for subtle warmth on hover (optional)
    - Overlay: black at 85% opacity over game world (pause/save screens)
  - **Fonts (3 fonts, all free, OFL/Apache licensed):**
    - **Bebas Neue** — Headers, menu buttons, titles. Condensed sans-serif, all caps. Industrial, RE2 energy.
      - Download: https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Bebas+Neue
      - Usage: Main menu items (36pt), pause menu items (42pt), screen titles (48-72pt)
      - Letter spacing: +200 to +400 (wider = more dramatic)
    - **Cormorant Garamond** — Body text, descriptions, save slot info. Elegant serif, SH2 literary horror.
      - Download: https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Cormorant+Garamond
      - Usage: Save descriptions (22pt), settings labels (24pt), dialog text (20pt)
      - Weights: Regular for body, SemiBold for emphasis
